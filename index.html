Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package in real-time on our shipping partner's website.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 150 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. International orders may be subject to customs duties.

Are all products authentic?

Absolutely. Every item in our collection is 100% authentic. We verify authenticity with each manufacturer and provide certificates of authenticity with premium items.

What is your return policy?

We offer hassle-free 30-day returns on most items. Products must be in original condition with packaging. See our Returns page for complete details.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! Premium gift wrapping is available for an additional fee. Select this option during checkout to present your gift beautifully.

How can I contact support?

Visit our Contact page or email support@eneshop.com. Our team responds within 24 hours on business days.

Data & Storage Management

Your shopping data (cart, wishlist, and newsletter subscriptions) is safely stored on your device using browser LocalStorage. Here's what you should know:

📦 What data is saved?

  • Shopping Cart: All products you add with quantity, size, and color
  • Wishlist (Favorites): Products you mark with the heart icon
  • Newsletter Emails: Email addresses for our newsletter

💾 How is it stored?

Your data is saved locally in your browser's storage, not on our servers. This means:

  • ✓ Only you can see your data on this device
  • ✓ Your data persists after closing the browser
  • ✓ Your data works offline
  • ⚠️ Data will be lost if you clear browser cache

🗑️ How to clear your data:

Option 1: Clear Cart Only

Go to your Shopping Cart and click the "Clear Cart" button. Only your shopping cart will be cleared.

Option 2: Reset Everything (Cart + Wishlist + Newsletter)

Click the button below to completely reset all your saved data on this device. This cannot be undone!

Option 3: Use Browser Developer Tools

Open your browser (F12 → Application → LocalStorage) and manually delete the keys:

  • eneshop_cart
  • eneshop_wishlist
  • eneshop_subscribers

Or simply clear your browser cache/cookies.

Returns & Exchanges

We make returns simple

30-Day Return Policy

Unsatisfied with your purchase? We offer a hassle-free 30-day money-back guarantee on most items. Here's how it works:

Return Process

  1. Contact our support team at support@eneshop.com with your order number
  2. Receive a prepaid shipping label
  3. Pack the item securely in original packaging
  4. Ship it back to us
  5. Receive your refund within 5-7 business days

Return Conditions

  • Item must be in original, unworn condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be included
  • Returns accepted within 30 days of purchase
  • Customized or personalized items may not be returnable

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We offer free exchanges with prepaid shipping labels. Simply contact our support team to arrange your exchange.
